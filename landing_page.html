Dual-Mode Command Capabilities

Combining the fidelity of orbital tracking with the utility of real-time impact simulation for actionable insights.

Core: Orbital Tracker (3D)

The **visualization engine** providing a simple, elegant 3D view of NEO trajectories and close-approach paths, directly driven by NASA orbital data.

Feature: Impact Simulator

The **simulation module** allowing users to select impact parameters (size, speed, location) to instantly calculate and visualize localized blast radius and seismic effects.

Mitigation Strategy Sandbox

Test deflection scenarios on NEOs like **Bennu** and instantly see the change in the asteroid's orbital path ($\Delta V$ needed) and reduced risk profile.

Risk Reporting & Analysis

Automatically calculate and display risk using the **Palermo and Torino Scales**, providing a single, universally understood metric for policymakers.

USGS Geospatial Layering

Overlay geographical data onto impact zones to identify critical infrastructure, population density, and tsunami risk for ocean impacts.

Developer Access

Export simulation data and visualizations via a simple, clean API structure, making integration into larger dashboards and documentation effortless.
